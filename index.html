About

DC204 is Winnipeg's chapter of DEF CON Groups — an informal, independent community of hackers, security researchers, hobbyists, and the curious. We meet to share knowledge, learn from each other, and build the kind of agency over our own systems and data that doesn't get handed to you by default.

No membership fees, no gatekeeping — just people in Winnipeg who care about security, privacy, and figuring out how things work.
